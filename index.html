Why it's different

Not a dashboard. Not a chatbot. The finished, provable answer.

vs. Citeline / IQVIA

We sell the answer, not a seat

Incumbents sell you a platform and analysts to interpret it. TrialGraph returns the finished memo — comparable trials, endpoints, competitors, failure patterns, readouts, risks — ready to forward to a board.

vs. naive AI / RAG

Reads every trial, can't bluff

RAG reasons over the top 5 results and hallucinates. TrialGraph aggregates the complete cohort, and a verifier checks every number and citation against source. No confident mistakes.

vs. the raw registry

We recover what it hides

~15% of terminations say "no reason given." We resolve them — sourced to SEC filings, FDA letters, and press — turning registry silence into cited cause.

What only recall-complete can see

Most trials don't die on biology. They die on money and enrollment — and you can't see that from the top 5 results.

Read all 1,159 terminated NSCLC trials and the pattern inverts everything: 61% stopped for funding or enrollment, only 15% for efficacy or safety. MASH: 44% financial. Alzheimer's is the exception that proves the method — there the science really does kill, 27% on efficacy. A sampling tool tells you "secure your endpoint." The complete read tells you to secure your funding and your sites.
